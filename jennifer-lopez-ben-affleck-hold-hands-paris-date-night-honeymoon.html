Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck Hold Hands on Parisian Date Night

July 15, 2024
blog

Newlyweds Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck are celebrating their honeymoon in Paris after surprising everyone by saying "I do" over the weekend.

The two were spotted enjoying a date night yesterday in the French city, accompanied by their children.

The blended family was pictured leaving a restaurant looking happy. Lopez brought the romance with a breezy red wrap dress and silver heart-shaped locket. She kept her beauty look bright and glowy, and held her hair up in a messy top knot. The Deep Water actor went for a classic navy suit, white dress shirt, and beige tie for his Parisian date with his new wife.

The pair held hands while leaving the restaurant—their kids following behind. (Affleck shares Violet, Samuel, and Seraphina with ex-wife Jennifer Garner, while Lopez shares twins Max and Emme with ex-husband Marc Anthony.)

DKPAR/MEGA

Affleck and Lopez—who married at A Little White Wedding Chapel in Vegas this past Saturday night—were also seen enjoying a Paris outing this morning.

The two were pictured showing sweet PDA on a private walk around a garden. The Marry Me star was glowing in a white flowy midi dress featuring a colorful floral print, while Affleck kept it cool in a sky-blue button-down, which he rolled up at the sleeves, and dark navy pants.

Mr. and Mrs. Affleck were photographed passionately kissing on a bench, surrounded by greenery, with Lopez sweetly embracing her new husband. They were also seen holding hands while walking around the garden—Affleck carrying a professional camera to capture their special moments.

Lopez confirmed the news of their marriage on her Instagram and in her newsletter, On the Jlo, and shared that she changed her last name to match her husband's. She also mentioned that she wore "a dress from an old movie" for part of the celebration—fans quickly realized it was the dress she posed in with Affleck for the poster of their 2004 romantic comedy, Jersey Girl.

"They were right when they said, 'all you need is love'. We are so grateful to have that in abundance, a new wonderful family of five amazing children and a life that we have never had more reason to look forward to," Lopez wrote in her newsletter. "Stick around long enough and maybe you’ll find the best moment of your life in a drive through in Las Vegas at twelve thirty in the morning in the tunnel of love drive through, with your kids and the one you’ll spend forever with. Love is a great thing, maybe the best of things-and worth waiting for."
