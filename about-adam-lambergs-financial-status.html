Adam Matthew Lamberg Background Information

Adam Matthew Lamberg, born on September 14, 1984, is an American actor, renowned for portraying David “Gordo” Gordon on the Disney Channel series Lizzie McGuire from 2001 to 2004. Lamberg hails from New York City, the son of Suzanne, a public high school teacher, and Marc Lamberg. His father’s heritage is Jewish, and his mother is of French Canadian descent. Lamberg identifies himself as a “cultural Jew.” Lamberg is no longer active in acting; instead, he pursued Geography at the University of California: Berkeley and now works as a development associate at the Irish Arts Center in New York City.
