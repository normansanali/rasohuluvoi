The businesswoman and author is the future wife of billionaire Amazon founder Jeff Bezos 

    Businesswoman, Emmy-winning journalist and now children's book author Lauren Sanchez – who you might also know as Amazon founder Jeff Bezos' fiancée – kicked off Oscars 2024 weekend on a glamorous note.

    Accompanied by her billionaire husband-to-be, Lauren, 54, poured herself a sexy, figure-hugging gown for the Chanel pre-Oscars bash on Saturday night. 

    During the party the duo mingled with famous names including Kerry Washington, Lily-Rose Depp and Kristen Stewart, as well as stars America Ferrera and Margot Robbie, who rocked a wild feather and miniskirt look.

    Complementing her look with glittering silver and diamond jewelry, strappy sandals and a pop of color with her neon pedicure, mom-of-three Lauren snuggled up to her fiancé, who looked stylish in a grey suit and black T-shirt.
