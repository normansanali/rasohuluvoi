Lil Baby Grabs Starlito For &quot;Exotic&quot;

October 01, 2024
blog

In a recent interview with Complex, Lil Baby revealed Starlito be one of his "favorite rappers." An artist who's never gotten the attention he deserved, it's not entirely surprising to hear that the younger voices in rap are looking to 'Lito as inspiration. Baby has some impressive features across his new project Harder Than Ever, but Starlito is easily the least obvious and most interesting selection. On "Exotic," it's clear that the Nashville rapper's style is complimentary to Baby's studied southern rap. In fact, learning that Baby has been a fan of Lito's for some time, it becomes easier to draw a line between their vocal styles. The record is heavy and high-BPM for both rappers standards, and they come through with braggy bars and stylish flows, but always bringing a unique energy to their rhymes.

Watch our recent interview with Lil Baby, in which he discusses working with Drake, Young Thug and the making of his debut album, below. He also discusses his features, which he says he selects very precisely. According to Baby, the person he'd like to work with the most is Atlanta legend Andre 3000.
