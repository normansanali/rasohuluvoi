Adele Enjoys a Romantic Italian Getaway with Boyfriend Rich Paul

September 21, 2024
blog

Adele was glowing this week while on a romantic vacation with her man.

The "Easy on Me" singer was all smiles yesterday while touring Porto Cervo, Italy, with boyfriend Rich Paul.

Adele looked radiant in a flowing, multicolored tunic dress and oversized black sunglasses. She accessorized with a stack of gold bracelets by Van Cleef & Arpels and carried a coveted summer accessory: an embroidered tote bag with black leather trim and handles by Saint Laurent.

She was seen shielding her face from the sun while next to Paul, who looked equally stylish in a white, fuchsia, navy, and yellow striped long-sleeved zip-up sweater worn over a white T-shirt and paired with white trousers. He, too, opted for gold accessories, including gold-tinted aviator sunglasses and a watch.

In another photo from the day, Adele was seen smiling while walking with friends.

BACKGRID

The singer—who finalized her divorce from husband-of-four-years Simon Konecki in March 2021—has been dating the sports agent since mid-2021. And yet, rumors of a possible engagement have already spread.

This February at the BRIT Awards, Adele was spotted wearing what looked to be a diamond ring, and in May, she shared a tribute to Paul that seemed to suggest the two bought a house or moved in together. The post featured photos of the pair's relationship, including one of them kissing and another of them sitting on a bench in matching gray tees. "Time flies," Adele simply captioned the post.
