Deadpool's Ryan Reynolds Kills Korean Karaoke While Dressed As Unicorn

August 15, 2024
blog

The release of Deadpool 2 is upon us. As the movie debut grows closer, Ryan Reynolds continues to find new ways to promote the film. His promo ranges from a signature corny banter to hilarious parodies and jokes, but this past weekend, Reynolds switched it up. Deadpool himself featured as a contestant on Korea TV's King of Mask Singer, which is a singing competition where the contestant wears a mask to conceal their identity while impressing the judges with their vocals. 

Reynolds dressed up as a unicorn, donning an entire costume instead of just a mask. His song of choice was "Tomorrow" from Annie, which he surprisingly reenacts perfectly. The 41-year-old actor hits the high notes, carries the long notes, and stays on key for the majority of the performance. If they didn't tell us it was Reynolds after the conclusion, it would still be obvious though. His voice is synonymous with Deadpool at this point, and the entire times he's singing, it sounds like a scene from the 'Merc with a Mouth's movie.
