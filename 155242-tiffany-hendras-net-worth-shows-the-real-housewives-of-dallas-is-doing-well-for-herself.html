Tiffany Hendra's Net Worth Shows 'The Real Housewives Of Dallas' Is Doing Well For Herself

July 22, 2024

It's no surprise that the ladies of The Real Housewives Of Dallas boast some pretty serious bank accounts. These women are among Dallas' social elite and they have the wallets to prove it. This city's cast is full of unique professions, from Dallas Cowboys cheerleader to surgical assistant. The Housewife with the most star power, though, is definitely Tiffany Hendra. And, star power is one thing, but what's Tiffany Hendra's RHOD net worth?

Well, Tiffany has had a career in entertainment that spans many more years than I had originally pegged her for. According to her Bravo profile, she has worked as an actress, TV host, and model in Los Angeles for over 15 years. She even had a starring/recurring role on a Cinemax series called, Black Tie Nights. Unfortunately, because the project was on Cinemax and included Tiffany going topless, she has been labeled as a "former adult film star" by a lot of media outlets. While she refutes that label, she doesn't shy away from the fact that she was involved in some racy projects. Tiffany told The Mr. Real Housewife that she has no regrets, saying, “So what I showed my t*ts and had love scenes, but what actress hasn’t? I did that series before I was married and it’s part of my journey. I own it.”

You go, girl. I think she's amazing for owning her past. And, she has certainly moved on to bigger and better things nowadays.

Tiffany's most obvious new source of income is her role on Real Housewives Of Dallas. According to Lalate news, each cast member is reportedly making $11,000 per episode. If true, that would mean Tiffany will be making $110,000 for the entire first season. It's Dallas' first season, so that's pretty good start.

After moving back to Dallas from Los Angeles to be closer to her family, Tiffany has thrown herself into her lifestyle blog and YouTube channel, Sanctuary Of Style. As part of her lifestyle brand, she is selling "Tiffirmation Tees" with inspiring quotes on them through her website. The shirts go for about $22-$26 and are actually really cute. New styles are promised on her site to be coming soon, too. The same site also showcases her husband's CDs and Aaron Hendra apparel for sale. So, she's definitely cashing in on her new notoriety. I don't blame her! Add that source of income to her bankroll.

According to net-worth.com, Tiffany reportedly has an estimated net worth of $1.8 million as of 2016. That's not even including the fact that she is married to a pretty successful musician whose star is also on the rise.

After 15 years of a successful career in the entertainment industry, it's no wonder that she has a pretty full wallet. Her net worth will only increase with her time as a Real Housewife.
