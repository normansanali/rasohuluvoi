Katie Holmes Wears a Vibrant Ulla Johnson Skirt &amp; Slouchy Boots

October 01, 2024
blog

Many of us are still living in lockdown amid the pandemic, but that doesn't mean that looking chic is completely off the table. In recent months, Katie Holmes has proven on multiple occasions that it's entirely possible to stay stylish while social distancing.

The actress was photographed in New York on Friday wearing a colorful outfit, showing that she's as ready for spring as the rest of us. And don't let Holmes's black overcoat fool you—the Dawson's Creek star revealed a pop of color in the form of a vibrant Ulla Johnson skirt underneath. The tiered skirt features a tie-dye print, and an asymmetrical sash, and is available to shop for $695. Fans of the fabric can also pick up a matching shirt from the designer.

Holmes paired the chic skirt with a white T-shirt, and slouchy black leather boots.

Holmes has been dating New York-based chef, Emilio Vitolo, throughout the pandemic, and the couple went Instagram official in late 2020. In celebration of the actress's birthday in December, Vitolo shared a sweet photo of them together and wrote, "The most amazing ,kindest, beautiful person ❤️. Every time I see your face it makes me smile . Happy Birthday !!! I love you !!"

In early March, Vitolo posted an Instagram photo of a snowman wearing a face mask in Central Park, and he tagged beau Holmes in the snap, too.

Clearly, the couple's romance is still going strong as just last week, Holmes and Vitolo stepped out together in New York with their arms linked.
