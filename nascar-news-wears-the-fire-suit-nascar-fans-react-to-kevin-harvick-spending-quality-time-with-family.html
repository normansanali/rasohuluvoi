With the 2022 NASCAR Cup season wrapped up, many drivers are spending the off-season differently. Obviously, some drivers are a little bored out of their skulls with little to no racing action. However, there are others who have taken advantage of the off-season to spend time with their families. Stewart-Haas Racing driver Kevin Harvick definitely falls in the latter category.

Recently, he posted some pictures on social media of himself hanging out with his family. This included his two children, Keelan and Piper, and his wife, DeLana. Keelan is already an active racer, but it appears that little Piper may soon follow her older brother and father.

How did NASCAR fans react to Kevin Harvick being with his family?

A lot of fans were happy to see the Harvick family spending time together. Some noted that DeLana was conspicuously missing from the photographs. Naturally, they wanted to know her reaction to having her daughter expressed an interest in racing. People also wondered whether DeLana Harvick managed to suit up and go for a drive.

One thing is for sure, several NASCAR fans are eager to see Keelan and Piper carry on the family tradition. As mentioned before, the older sibling is already an active racer. Meanwhile, his sister is just content with taking baby steps and may soon try to try her hand at motor racing.
